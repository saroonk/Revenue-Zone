Meet Our Founder

Mr. Sreejith

Founder & CEO, Revenue Zone

Revenue Zone was founded by Mr. Sreejith, a seasoned pioneer in the OTA (Online Travel Agent) management industry, with over 19 years of expertise. Since 2006, Mr. Sreejith has been a driving force behind the success of several prominent hotel groups in Kerala, including Somatheeram Group, Emarald Group, Stride Hotels Group, and renowned Ayurveda resorts like Punarnava Ayurveda and Thaqdees Ayurveda. His extensive experience in OTA management has helped these industry leaders improve their online presence and significantly increase their bookings.

Before launching Revenue Zone, Mr. Sreejith honed his skills as a Project Manager at Digital Factory Revenue Factory, one of Kerala’s leading digital marketing and OTA management companies. There, he furthered his expertise in Digital Marketing and Revenue Management, helping many businesses navigate the complexities of the digital era and achieve remarkable growth.

The Values We Live By

Fueling Your Growth

At Revenue Zone, our mission is simple yet powerful: to extend the benefits of OTA Management services and Digital Marketing expertise to all hotels, resorts, and accommodations across Kerala. As Kerala’s tourism industry grows, especially in heritage centres and local tourism hubs, many property owners are still unaware of how to manage their online presence effectively.

The Kerala Government's tourism promotion initiatives are opening new doors for the tourism industry, and we are here to guide property owners on how to take full advantage of these opportunities. With the right OTA management strategy, every hotel and resort – no matter how big or small – can thrive in today’s digital marketplace.
